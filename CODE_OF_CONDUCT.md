# OpenZues Code of Conduct

We want OpenZues to feel ambitious, technical, and welcoming.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but stay honest about problems and risks.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and product choices, not on people.
- Share context that helps others reproduce issues and move work forward.
- Leave the project more understandable than you found it.

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- hate speech or discriminatory language
- doxxing, threats, or unwanted sexual attention
- deliberately misleading reports, hostile pile-ons, or bad-faith review behavior
- publishing private security details or secrets irresponsibly

## Moderation

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these expectations and may restrict
participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.

## Reporting

If you run into conduct problems, report them through GitHub in the least public way available and include only
the context needed to understand what happened.
